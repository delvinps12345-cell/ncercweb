About the Institution

Nehru College of Engineering
and Research Centre

Nehru College of Engineering and Research Centre (NCERC) is a premier technical institution affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), located at Pampady, Thrissur, Kerala. Established in 2001, the college is managed by the Nehru Group of Institutions.

NCERC is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple engineering and technology disciplines, with a strong focus on research and industry collaboration.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, active student clubs, and an excellent placement record, NCERC is committed to producing industry-ready graduates who contribute meaningfully to society and technology.

Core Features
Everything You Need to Manage Your Workforce Records
Designed specifically for NCERC with secure architecture, clean data flow, and an intelligent AI-powered admin interface.
Employee Self-Entry Form
Employees fill a structured multi-step form with personal, contact, and professional details. All data is validated before submission.
Secure Admin Login
JWT-based authentication ensures only authorized NCERC administrators can access the dashboard and employee records.
Admin Dashboard
Comprehensive table view of all submitted records with search, sort, filter, and export capabilities in one clean interface.
Persistent Database Storage
All employee data stored securely in Firebase Firestore or MySQL. Records are never lost and always retrievable.
Search and Filter Records
Filter by department, designation, or employment type. Search by name or employee ID with instant real-time results.
Workflow
How the System Works
A simple three-step process from employee submission to admin review.
01
Employee Submits Form
The employee fills in personal, contact, and professional details through the web form and clicks submit.
02
Data Stored in Database
Validated and sanitized data is securely stored in the central database with a unique employee record ID.
03
Admin Reviews Records
The administrator logs into the secure dashboard to view, search, filter, and manage all employee records.
System Roles
Two Access Levels, One Unified System
Employee
Employee Access
Employees use the public-facing form to submit their details. No login required — just fill and submit.
  • Fill personal information form
  • Submit contact details
  • Enter professional information
  • Upload profile photo
  • Receive submission confirmation
Administrator
Admin Access
Administrators have full control over the system via a protected dashboard accessible only with valid credentials.
  • Secure login with JWT session
  • View all employee records
  • Search, filter, and sort data
  • View detailed employee profiles
  • Export records to CSV or PDF
  • Delete or manage records
